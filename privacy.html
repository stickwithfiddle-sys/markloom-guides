Markloom — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Markloom ("we", "us") makes minimalist wall art and original quote apparel, sold through our shop at markloom.etsy.com. This policy explains what data our software — including our TikTok integration — accesses, and how we handle it.

1. Who this covers

Our TikTok integration is used by Markloom to publish our own marketing videos to our own TikTok account. We do not use it to collect, access, or process data belonging to other TikTok users, your followers, or the public.

2. What we access via TikTok

  • Basic account info (scope user.info.basic): the open ID and display name of the connected Markloom account, used only to confirm the correct account is connected.
  • Content posting (scopes video.upload, video.publish): permission to upload our own marketing videos to our account, as private drafts for our review.

We do not request or access your videos, messages, contacts, analytics about other users, or any data about people other than the account owner who connects the app.

3. How we store and use it

Authorization tokens are stored locally on the account owner's own computer (in a local environment file) and are used solely to upload our marketing content. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising profiling. Uploaded videos are our own original product marketing.

4. Retention and deletion

Tokens are kept only while the integration is in use and can be revoked at any time from the connected TikTok account's app settings, or by deleting the local environment file. Revoking access immediately stops all use.

5. AI-generated content

Some of our marketing videos are produced with AI tools. We disclose this on each post using TikTok's AI-generated-content label, in line with TikTok's policies.

6. Contact

Questions or requests: message us through our Etsy shop at markloom.etsy.com.

Markloom is an independent small business. This integration uses the TikTok API in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms.
